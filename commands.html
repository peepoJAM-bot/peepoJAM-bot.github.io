Commands
Find out the commands with "!help"



!help

Displays all the commands and descriptions

!invite

Sends bot invite link in DMs

!loop (l)

Toggle music loop

!lyrics (ly)

Get lyrics for the currently playing song

!move (mv)

Move songs around in the queue

!np

Show now playing song

!pause

Pause the currently playing song

!ping

Show the bot's average ping

!play (p)

Plays audio from YouTube (Soundcloud support coming soon)

!playlist (pl)

Play a playlist from YouTube

!pruning

Toggle pruning of bot messages

!queue (q)

Show the music queue and now playing

!remove (rm)

Remove song from the queue

!resume (r)

Resume currently playing music

!search

Search and select videos to play

!shuffle

Shuffles queue

!skip (s)

Skips the currently palying song

!skipto (st)

Skip to the selected queue number

!stop

Stops the music

!uptime

Shows bot uptime

!volume

Change volume of currently playing music
